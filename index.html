About ARCALYX

The ULTIMATE Sandbox Survival.

The Earth is dying. The stars are waiting. After the crystal ARCALYX shattered into our planet, its splinters and shards birthed strange, hostile biomes and catastrophic storms.

It is up to you. You decide:

  • Will you fight through each corrupted biome, defeat world-shaping bosses, and restore balance?
  • Or escape into space, build ships, colonize planets, and leave Earth behind?
